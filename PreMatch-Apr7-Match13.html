3 Facts Worth Knowing
Numbers that tell the story today
1
Sooryavanshi has never faced Bumrah in professional cricket. Tonight is the first.
52 off 17 (SR 305.9) on debut. 31 off 18 in Game 2. He hits the ball so casually you’d think Bumrah was a gentle medium pacer at a club match. He isn’t. Bumrah takes a wicket every 13.5 balls in IPL since 2024. Something will give. That’s going to be the moment of this tournament.
2
Barsapara dismissed CSK for 127 six days ago. Same pitch. Same dewy night.
Early moisture, swing off the surface, and a crowd that turns up just to watch the fast bowlers. RR chased 128 with 8 wickets to spare. MI have never played here. They’re walking into the lion’s den and the lion just had a very satisfying meal.
3
Tonight is MI’s first-ever game at Barsapara — their 31st different IPL venue.
RR know every blade of grass, every moisture patch, every shortish delivery that rears off a length. MI are parachuting in blind with a returning captain and a prayer. History suggests the home side wins the information war before the toss is even called.
