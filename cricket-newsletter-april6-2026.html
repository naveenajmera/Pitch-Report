Women’s Cricket Fun Fact

Women’s Cricket · Record Watch
200*
Laura Wolvaardt — First Women’s ODI Double Century
In January 2024, South Africa’s Laura Wolvaardt scored 200* off 151 balls against West Indies Women — becoming the first batter in women’s ODI history to ever score a double century. She hit 20 fours and 8 sixes. The innings broke a ceiling that had stood unchallenged across 47 years of women’s ODI cricket. The previous highest individual score was 188*. Wolvaardt didn’t just break the record — she did it by 12 runs in one extraordinary innings at Cape Town.
