3 Facts from Last Night
Numbers you’ll be quoting all day
1
Bumrah has now gone 3 successive IPL games without a wicket.
He finished 0-32 in 3 overs. A 15-year-old battered him for two sixes and a four in those same overs. The greatest T20 bowler alive is not having a great April. The greatest T20 opener alive is making sure of that.
2
RR’s powerplay: 59/0. MI’s powerplay: 29/3.
Same pitch. Same conditions. Same evening. The difference was a 15-year-old and a man from Mumbai who plays like he’s personally offended by every delivery. MI’s top three were back in the dugout before Jaiswal had even broken sweat.
3
RR are 3-0 in 2026. The only unbeaten team left.
They haven’t lost a match this season. Not against DC. Not against CSK. Not against MI. At this rate, Guwahati is fast becoming their fortress — which is remarkable given they’ve only played here twice in history.
