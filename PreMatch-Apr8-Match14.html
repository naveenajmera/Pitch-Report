3 Facts Worth Knowing
Numbers that will define tonight’s contest
1
KL Rahul has 1 run in IPL 2026 — but owns Siraj: 135 runs off 79 balls, SR 170.88.
GT might hold Siraj back to deny Rahul a free ride. Instead they’d bring on Rabada — who has dismissed Rahul 3 times in 12 innings at an average of 22.66. Whichever way GT go, it’s a tactical toss-up that could set the match’s tempo inside the powerplay.
2
Sameer Rizvi is one POTM award from making history. Four in a row has never been done in IPL.
He became the 7th player ever to win three consecutive POTM awards with his 90* vs MI. The streak started in DC’s final match of IPL 2025. A fourth would make him the first ever in the 19-year history of the tournament to achieve this — beating Kohli, Sehwag, Kallis and company.
3
Arun Jaitley Pitch #5: Run rate 10.11, death-over rate 12.67 in recent night games.
This pitch has hosted 4 night games in IPL 2024–25. It plays big. The death overs explode. Any score under 175 is likely a par total on this deck — unless the moisture from this afternoon’s rain assists the seamers early and changes the equation entirely.
